ABOUT US

At Irbis Tile & Construction Inc. we specialize in tile installation and porcelain slab fabrication projects proudly serving Toronto and GTA. From small residential renovations, custom built homes to large-scale commercial projects, our portfolio showcases a diverse range of successful installations.

International Experience & Excellent Craftsmanship

FAQ

CONTAS US

What types of tile do you install?

>

We install a wide variety of tiles, including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, and glass tiles.

How do I know if tile installation is right for my project?

>

Tile installation is ideal for areas that require durability and easy maintenance, such as kitchens, bathrooms, and entryways.

Do you offer free estimates and how soon will I hear back from you?

>

Yes, we offer free estimates. You can expect to hear back from us within 24 hours.

How long will my tile installation take?

>

The duration of the installation depends on the size and complexity of the project, but most installations are completed within 1-3 days.

Can you work with other contractors or corporate clients?

>

Yes, we frequently collaborate with other contractors and corporate clients to ensure seamless project execution.

Do you offer any warranties on your work?

>

Yes, we offer a 5-year warranty on all our tile installation work.
